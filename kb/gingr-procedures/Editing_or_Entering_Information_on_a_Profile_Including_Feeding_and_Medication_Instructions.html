Editing or Entering Information on a Profile Including Feeding and Medication Instructions

Operating Procedure


Anytime anything changes with a pet, it is vital to update the animal's profile to reflect the new information. 

 

Editing or Entering Information on a profile:

  • Navigate to the pet's profile on Gingr.
  • Press the pencil button, select either 'Edit Owner' or 'Edit (pet's name)', depending on what you want to edit.
  • This screen will give you complete access to view, edit, or add any client information. Once you are done with what you needed to do,
  • Press the 'Save' button at the bottom of the page. Otherwise, it will not be saved.

ADDING OR EDITING FEEDING INFORMATION:

  • Press the '+' button to add a new meal, and use the 'x' button to remove a meal. Simply deleting the text wont remove a meal - you need to press the 'x' button to completely delete the meal slot. If you need to change the feeding information that is already there, simply type over the existing text. Use caution and triple-check to make sure the feeding information is correct.

ADDING OR EDITING MEDICATION INFORMATION:

  • To add medications, press the plus button to add each medication, add quantity, unit and medication instructions.) To delete medications, you need to press the 'x' button next to the medication slot. Use caution and triple-check to make sure the medication information is correct.

    • You may also add specific date(s) if the medication is only for a certain time period.

IF THERE ARE ANY QUESTIONS ON HOW TO DO THIS, ASK A SUPERVISOR. ACCURATE FEEDING AND MEDICATION INFORMATION IS OF UPMOST IMPORTANCE.

 

Adding a Card on file (without a payment being processed):

  1. Navigate to the 'Edit Owner' screen.
  2. Locate the credit card section, and enter the credit card details.
  3. Press "Use this Card" AND THEN Press the Save button at the bottom of the page. If you do not press BOTH BUTTONS IN CORRECT ORDER, THE CREDIT CARD WILL NOT SAVE.
