Pulling Up Daycare List and Arrival and Departure Reports

Operating Procedure

 

This procedure show you how to pull up daycare lists and boarding arrival and departure reports.

 

Daycare Report:

  1. Navigate to 'Reports & More' > 'Reports' > 'Reservations By Day'.
  2. Select the desired date.
  3. In the 'Reservation Types' section, select only the daycare options.
  4. Press 'Generate Report'.
  5. "X" out of either the 'Check Ins' or 'Check Outs' section. Since daycares are for only one day, we must "X" out of one of them, otherwise the report will be duplicated.
  6. Then, press 'Print PDF' to print.

 

Boarding Arrival Report and Departure Report:

  1. Navigate to 'Reports & More' > 'Reports' > 'Reservations By Day'.
  2. Select the desired date.
  3. In the 'Reservation Types' section, select only the boarding options.
  4. Press 'Generate Report'.
  5. This will generate both a 'Check Ins' and a 'Check Outs' section.
    • To see only departures: "X" out of the 'Check Ins' section.
    • To see only arrivals: "X" out of the 'Check Outs' section.
  6. Then, press 'Print PDF' to print.
