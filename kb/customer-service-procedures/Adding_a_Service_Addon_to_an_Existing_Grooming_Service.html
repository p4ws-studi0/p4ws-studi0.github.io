Adding a Service Add-on to an Existing Grooming Service

Operating Procedure

 

Adding a service add-on to an existing grooming service:

  • Navigate to the desired reservation or appointment.
  • Open the 'Services' tab.
  • Select the menu button (three lines, looks like a hamburger), next to the grooming service.
  • Select 'Edit Service'.
  • Select the desired service add-on(s)
  • Press 'Save Changes!' at the bottom of the screen to save.
