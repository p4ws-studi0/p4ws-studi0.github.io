BOARDING:

 

In general, we recommend the earlier, the better, since it allows us to start playtime earlier, in addition to other factors. 

  • Monday-Friday: 9AM to 5PM
    • Clients can drop off as soon as 7AM, but to avoid the daycare rush, we recommend the hours above.
    • Can drop off past 5PM if there is management approval.
  • Saturday: 9AM to 5PM
  • Sunday: 9AM-11AM, 3PM-5PM.

 

DAYCARE:

 

  • Monday-Friday: Drop-off and pickup can be anytime from 7AM to 7PM.
    • Recommended hours are: 7AM-9AM Drop-off, 3PM-7PM Pick-up to ensure pet receives all playtimes. 
  • Saturday: 9AM to 5PM
  • Sunday: 9AM-11AM, 3PM-5PM. (Note: we cannot do half-days on Sundays, as we are closed in the middle of the day.)
