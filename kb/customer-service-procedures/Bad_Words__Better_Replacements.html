Bad Words & Better Replacements

Overview

 

It is imperative to paint a positive picture of our exceptional care by choosing the right words. This overview will outline words that should never be used, and better replacements for them.

 

 

 

"Bad" Word > Instead, use: Better Replacement
No (Your'e Wrong) > Instead, use: Actually
No (Can't/Won't) > Instead, use: Wish we could
Problem/Trouble > Instead, use: Questions
Kennel > Instead, use: Sleeping Area/Lodging Area
Cage > Instead, use: Suite/Enclosure
Boarding > Instead, use: Lodging/Overnight Accomodations
Picky Eater > Instead, use: Selective, nibbler
Kennel Cough > Instead, use: Canine Cough
Isolation > Instead, use: Private Area
Price Increase > Instead, use: Price Adjustments
Calm Down > Instead, use: I'm sure I can help
Fixed > Instead, use: Neutered/Spayed
Stress > Instead, use: Sensitive
Thanks > Instead, use: Thank you
Out of Control Dog > Instead, use: Challenging
Shots > Instead, use: Vaccinations
Can't > Instead, use: What we can do
Well > Instead, use: Don't say it! 
Huh? > Instead, use: Excuse me
Ward > Instead, use: Wing
Fat > Instead, use: Spoiled/Pampered
Hyper > Instead, use: Energetic
Cancelation > Instead, use: Opening
Fine > Instead, use: Great, I'm Terrific, Excellent! 
Ok, Yeah, Yes > Instead, use: Absolutely, Certainly, My Pleasure, Of Course, I'd Be Happy To
