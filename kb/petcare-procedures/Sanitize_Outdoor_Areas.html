Sanitize Outdoor Areas

Operating Procedure

Cleaning the Yards after Sessions:

 

  • Sanitize with 256 both indoors & all outside yards – This should be done every day. Patios with concrete should not be cleaned during freezing temps.

  • Empty the poop buckets from the play gym or greenyard and empty and refill scoop water buckets with foamer. 

  • Windex windows and clean off window sills and any surface that may collect dust or dirt.

  • Clean out the gym drain bucket Monday & Thursday (twice per week).
