Social Media Posts

 

Operating Procedure

 

We post to Facebook and Instagram on an almost daily basis. When posting to social media, please only select the best photos from the day. For Facebook, it should be around 10-15 photos, and on Instagram, 3-5 photos is a good range. If there are more wonderful photos taken, don't hesitate to post more pictures than these recommendations - but still remember to only post the best photos out of the bunch. Do not post a photo in which you can read a dog's ID collar, as we want to maintain all pet's anonymity online for safety. Please use fun but appropriate captions - some good ideas include the day of the week (ie: "Sunday Funday!) or national days relating to dogs (ie: National Pet Day, etc).
