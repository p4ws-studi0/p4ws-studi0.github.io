Cultivating a 'Paws'itive Culture

 

 

 

Our Mission:

 

To enhance the lives of pets and their owners by providing outstanding care.

 

8 Core Values:

  • Respect to animals and coworkers

  • Integrity

  • Accountability

  • Adaptability and willingness to change

  • Communication

  • Innovation

  • Excellence in care

  • Growth mindset

 

Our objective is to instill a culture where every team member prioritizes the well-being and satisfaction of our pets and customers above all else. Regardless of personal feelings or challenges, we commit to setting aside any distractions at the door and dedicating ourselves wholeheartedly to delivering the best possible experience for both the animals and customers in our care every single day. 

 

Paws is what we make it to be. Our continuous goal is to create a stress-free culture where it is enjoyable for both pets and team members, while ensuring we are safe and adhering to our rules, procedures and values.
