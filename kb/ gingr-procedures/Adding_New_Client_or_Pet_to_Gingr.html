Adding New Client or Pet to Gingr

Operating Procdure

 

This procedure will outline how to add a new client, or a new pet to an existing client file on Gingr.

Adding a pet to an existing file

  1. Navigate to the desired owner's file on Gingr.
  2. Press the pencil icon on the top right of the screen, then click 'Edit Owner'.
  3. Once in the editing owner screen, press 'Add Another Animal' at the top of the screen.
  4. Use company script to fill out pet information accurately. 
  5. Once file is complete, press 'Save' at the bottom of page to save.
