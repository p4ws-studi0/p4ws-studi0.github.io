Pulling Up Service Reports (Including Grooming and Activities)

Operating Procedure

 

This procedure will show you how to pull up grooming reports, activity package reports, and more.

 

  1. Navigate to 'Reports & More' > 'Reports' > 'Services By Date'.
  2. Select the desired date(s).
  3. In the 'Services' section, select the desired service option(s).
  4. Press 'Scheduled Within Date Range' - otherwise the report will show up incorrectly.
  • If you are pulling up services from past dates, select 'Include Checked Out Reservations?'
  1. Press 'Generate Report!'.
  2. Then, press 'Print PDF' to print.
