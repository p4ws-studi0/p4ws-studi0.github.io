Making Animal or Owner Notes

Operating Procedure

 

There are two ways to make animal or owner notes in Gingr:

 

Method One

  1. Navigate to the desired animal or owner profile.
  2. Open up the 'Employee Notes' tab.
  3. Write an in-depth, detailed note.
  4. Initial your name at the end of the note once you are done.
  5. Press 'Save Note' to save.

 

Method Two

  1. Navigate to the desired animal or owner profile.
  2. Next to the animal's name, click the clipboard icon.
  3. This notes 'pop-up' screen allows you to switch between owner notes and animal notes easily.
