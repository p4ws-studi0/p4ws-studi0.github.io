Bedtime Story Tuck-in

Operating Procedure

 

The bedtime story tuck-ins are started before the nightly Lock-Up and should take 3-5 minutes each. This is exclusively included in the Gold package for our overnight guests. 

 

 

  • Grab a children’s book.
  • Sit with the pet and read the book.
  • Pet the dog or cat, straighten the bedding, top the water, say goodnight.
  • Please conduct a quick wellness check to assure the pet is healthy and happy before sleep.
  • Sign off on the play list after you do it with the time completed.
